Upper left subwindow:
      Shows the series of commits to this repository in chronological order.
      It also shows a hierarchical tree of commits along with branches.
      Information displayed:
      		  commit messages
		  remote branches
		  commit SHA
		  and current commit

Upper Middle subwindow:
      Shows the name and emails of the authors or contributors to the repo.
      Each row of authors correspond to the commits they made on the left
      subwindow.

Upper Right subwindow:
      Shows the corresponding commits dates of each role of commits.

Lower Left subwindow:
      Shows the corresponding changes done by a commit in diff format.
      
Lower Right subwindow:
      Shows files impacted by the selected commit.
